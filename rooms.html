  • Central hub

    The central hub in a space station is used for connecting different modules and vehicles, providing power and communication, and enabling various experiments and activities. It is similar to a central hub in a station on Earth, but in space.

  • 2 Docking ports

    Docking ports are devices that allow two spacecraft to join together in space. They are used for various purposes, such as transferring crew, cargo, fuel, or power between spacecraft, or creating larger structures like space stations. Docking ports are essential for space exploration and operations, as they enable communication, cooperation, and resource sharing among different spacecraft and space agencies.

  • Habitation ring

    A habitation ring is a part of a space hotel that provides living quarters and amenities for the guests and crew. It is usually located on the outermost part of the space station, and rotates to create artificial gravity for the occupants. The habitation ring will also have windows that offer spectacular views of Earth and space. A habitation ring is designed to make the space hotel comfortable and enjoyable for the visitors, while also providing the necessary facilities and services for their safety and well-being.

  • Hab modules

    Hab modules are short for habitation modules, which are the parts of a space hotel that provide living quarters and amenities for the guests and crew. They are usually pressurized and contain beds, bathrooms, kitchens, windows, and other facilities to make the stay comfortable and enjoyable. Hab modules are essential for space hotels, as they provide the necessary space and comfort for the visitors, while also meeting the safety and environmental requirements of living in space.

  • Solar panels

    Solar panels are devices that convert sunlight into electricity. Solar panels are essential for our space hotel to operate, as they would provide the main source of electricity for the station. The solar panels are attached to the exterior of the station, where they would receive constant sunlight and generate power. The power they recieved then be distributed to the various systems and modules of the station, such as life support, lighting, communication, entertainment and propulsion. Our solar panels also help reduce the dependence on resupply missions from Earth. The solar panels used for our space hotel are lightweight, efficient, durable and reliable. They also designed to withstand the harsh environment of space and to fold or deploy as needed.
