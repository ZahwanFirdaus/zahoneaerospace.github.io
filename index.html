Polaris Space Hotel consisted of a spinning habitation ring around a central hub, providing artificial spin gravity for its guests and crew. The rotating ring consisted of a continuous hallway with 4 elevators to the hub and modules attached to the side facing Earth. These modules were used for the guest rooms and other hotel facilities, as well as for the flight deck, the control room of the station.
